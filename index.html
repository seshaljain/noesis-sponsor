Noesis 7.0

Vision's Annual Event

Get Started

NOESIS is the Signature Event of Vision. It was started in year 2011 and continues to be the biggest technical event of the Institute after Technosearch. It is a 2 Day Mega Technical Event conducted in February every year. It sees enthusiastic participation from multiple colleges, with over 1000 participants each year.

Branch-specific Technical Workshops are conducted to acquaint students with recent advancements in their branch, with hands-on partners to help them build experience to give them an edge over others.

Month: February | Branch-wise | Location: NTB, MANIT
